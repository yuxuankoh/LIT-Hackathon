Regulations
  • From 10 August, retail establishments have to ensure 1m spacing between groups of up to 5 persons.
  • From 10 August, establishments can only provide “mask-off” personal care services (e.g. facial, make-up services, saunas) to customers who are:
    • Fully vaccinated
    • Recovered from COVID-19 and can provide a valid Pre-Event Test (PET) exemption notice.
    • Have a valid negative PET result for the duration of his/her dine-in from an MOH-approved COVID-19 test provider within the last 24 hours.
