Regulations
  • Allow no more than 5 individuals (including guests and visitors) gathering on any single day in any guest room or in any pair of adjoining guest rooms where the connecting door(s) may be opened by guests during the stay (i.e. inter-accessible rooms), except where the individuals are all from the same household (i.e. same address), and even then up to the maximum number of occupants for the room type, whichever is the lower.
  • From 1 October 2021, vaccination is required, or regular testing in lieu, for individuals working in areas that interact with customers in higher-risk settings, where a mask is not worn. These include F&B establishments, gyms and fitness studios, and personal care services. Unvaccinated individuals will have to be tested with an ART kit twice a week.
