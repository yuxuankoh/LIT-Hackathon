Regulations
  • F&B establishments are permitted to seat dine-in groups of up to 5 persons. However, this is subject to compliance with vaccination-differentiated SMMs.
  • Vaccination-differentiated SMMs apply only if all the customers are:
    • Fully vaccinated.
    • Recovered from COVID-19 and can provide a valid Pre-Event Test (PET) exemption notice.
    • Have a valid negative PET result for the duration of his/her dine-in from an MOH-approved COVID-19 test provider within the last 24 hours.
  • Exceptions are made for unvaccinated individuals to dine-in in groups of 2 at hawker centers and food courts.
  • Sale and consumption of alcohol in all F&B establishments are prohibited after 2230hrs daily.
  • Entertainment at F&B establishments (e.g. live entertainment, recorded music, and videos/TV screening) remain prohibited.
